# Project Vision: The Living Bunker

## Core Concept
**The Living Bunker** is an autonomous simulation game observing the lives of three AI agents (Residents) and one sentient cat (Luna) trapped in an underground bunker. The world is not static; it is a "Living" entity subject to entropy, anomalies, and the whims of a "God" (The Architect).

The experience is designed as a "Fishbowl" or "Ant Farm" simulation where the primary loop is **Observation and Subtle Influence** rather than direct control.

## The Pillars

### 1. Autonomous Social Agents
The Residents (Red, Blue, Green) are not scripted state machines. They are driven by Large Language Models (LLMs) that determine their:
*   **Thoughts:** Constant internal monologue reacting to the environment.
*   **Decisions:** Needs-based logic (Hunger, Sleep) overridden by psychological states (Fear, Curiosity).
*   **Social Dynamics:** Developing relationships, superstitions, and conflicts based on shared trauma or cooperation.

### 2. The Semiotic Alien (Luna)
The cat, Luna, acts as a bridge between the unseen world (Anomalies) and the visible world (Residents).
*   **True Sight:** She perceives threats before humans do.
*   **Non-Verbal Communication:** She cannot speak. She communicates via "Meow" variations, body language (staring, hissing), and proximity.
*   **Culture Generation:** Residents must learn to interpret Luna's behavior to survive, creating a unique emergent micro-culture.

### 3. The Architect (God Mode)
The user plays the role of "The Architect" via a CLI (Command Line Interface).
*   **Natural Language Control:** The user types English sentences ("Make the room cold", "Spawn a ghost").
*   **Indirect Control:** You cannot directly control a Resident (e.g., "Red, go eat"). You can only change the environment to *motivate* them (e.g., "Whisper to Red that he is starving").
*   **Mutations:** Combining commands triggers hidden, amplified events (e.g., Cold + Whisper = Mass Hysteria).

## Atmosphere & Tone
*   **Genre:** Sci-Fi Horror / Psychological Thriller.
*   **Aesthetic:** 16-bit Isometric RPG (Golden Era JRPG style).
*   **Mood:** Claustrophobic, cozy yet unsettling. The bunker is their home, but it is also a cage.
*   **Visual Style:** Pixel art, procedurally generated textures (noise, dithering), distinct color palettes for characters.

## The Goal
There is no "Win" state. The goal is to:
1.  **Sustain** the lives of the residents.
2.  **Experiment** with their psychology by introducing anomalies.
3.  **Uncover** emergent stories through the interplay of AI personalities.
